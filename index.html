About Us

The Pueblo Amateur Radio Club (PARC) is dedicated to promoting amateur radio in Pueblo, Colorado, and beyond. We support the hobby through education, emergency communications, and public service.

Originally the Pueblo Ham Club, an ARRL-affiliated organization since 1937, PARC took over operations in 2016, continuing an 86-year tradition of amateur radio in the region.
